Mucota rebonding

  •   Reduce hair frizziness
  •   Hair hydration
  •   Soft rebonding effect
Process
  1. 1. Hair wash
    Wash away any impurities

  3. 3. Let your Hair Set In
    Hair goes through the oxidation process

Melon Beauty salon history

Melon Beauty Salon was established in 1989, originally opening in a modest storefront at 682 Hougang Ave 8. It later expanded to Hougang Central Block 811, #01-192. During construction of our Star of Kovan outlet, we temporarily relocated to 1187 Upper Serangoon Rd, #01-52. By 2021, we had moved to our permanent location at Star of Kovan. Throughout the years, our core mission has remained consistent: to offer a one-stop service in a welcoming and comfortable environment where our customers can be pampered and well taken care of. At Melon Beauty Salon, we exclusively use all-natural products
